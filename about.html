FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW

SHOPPING

HOW LONG DOES IT TAKE TO GET MY ORDER?

ORDERS ARE TYPICALLY PROCESSED WITHIN 1-2 BUSINESS DAYS. DELIVERY WITHIN HARARE TAKES 2-3 BUSINESS DAYS. FOR COLLECTION ORDERS, YOU'LL BE NOTIFIED WHEN YOUR ORDER IS READY FOR PICKUP.

WHAT DOES BALLYLIKE MEAN?

BALLYLIKE* REPRESENTS A MOVEMENT WHERE FAITH MEETS FASHION. IT'S ABOUT EXPRESSING YOUR BELIEFS BOLDLY THROUGH STYLE, CELEBRATING CULTURE, AND EMBRACING ARTISTIC EXPRESSION IN ATHLETIC FASHION.

HOW DO I ORDER?

BROWSE OUR COLLECTIONS, SELECT YOUR DESIRED ITEMS, ADD THEM TO YOUR CART, AND PROCEED TO CHECKOUT. YOU CAN CHOOSE BETWEEN HOME DELIVERY OR COLLECTION AT OUR STORE IN HARARE.

PAYMENT

WHAT PAYMENT METHODS DO YOU ACCEPT?

WE ACCEPT MULTIPLE PAYMENT METHODS INCLUDING MOBILE MONEY (ECOCASH, ONEMONEY), BANK TRANSFERS, AND CASH ON DELIVERY/COLLECTION. ONLINE PAYMENT OPTIONS ARE ALSO AVAILABLE AT CHECKOUT.

CAN I PAY WHEN I COLLECT MY ORDER?

YES! CASH ON COLLECTION IS AVAILABLE. SIMPLY SELECT THIS OPTION DURING CHECKOUT AND PAY WHEN YOU PICK UP YOUR ORDER AT OUR HARARE LOCATION.

ARE ONLINE PAYMENT OPTIONS AVAILABLE?

YES, WE OFFER SECURE ONLINE PAYMENT OPTIONS INCLUDING MOBILE MONEY AND DIGITAL WALLETS FOR YOUR CONVENIENCE.

SHIPPING & DELIVERY

WHERE CAN I COLLECT MY ORDER?

ORDERS CAN BE COLLECTED AT OUR STORE IN HARARE, ZIMBABWE. YOU'LL RECEIVE THE EXACT ADDRESS AND COLLECTION INSTRUCTIONS VIA EMAIL OR SMS WHEN YOUR ORDER IS READY.

DO YOU DELIVER WITHIN HARARE?

YES! WE OFFER DELIVERY SERVICES THROUGHOUT HARARE. DELIVERY FEES VARY BASED ON YOUR LOCATION. SELECT DELIVERY AT CHECKOUT TO SEE THE COST FOR YOUR AREA.

HOW DO I TRACK MY DELIVERY OR COLLECTION STATUS?

YOU'LL RECEIVE SMS AND EMAIL UPDATES AT EVERY STAGE: ORDER CONFIRMATION, PROCESSING, READY FOR COLLECTION/OUT FOR DELIVERY, AND COMPLETION. YOU CAN ALSO CONTACT US DIRECTLY FOR REAL-TIME UPDATES.
