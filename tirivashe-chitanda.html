Founder Profile

Tirivashe Chitanda

Founder of Ballylike

Tirivashe Chitanda is a Zimbabwean creative entrepreneur and the founder of Ballylike, a fashion and lifestyle brand shaped by minimal design, urban culture, and artistic expression. Through Ballylike, he focuses on creating products that represent individuality, ambition, and modern African creativity.
