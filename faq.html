Frequently Asked Questions

Shopping

How long does it take to get my order?

Orders are usually ready within 2–5 working days depending on availability and payment confirmation. Express same-day options may be available in Harare.

What does Ballylike mean?

“Ballylike” comes from a local slang expression meaning *to do something in an outstanding, stylish, and memorable way*. It represents our philosophy — that shopping should always feel exceptional, whether it’s the products you buy or the service you receive.

How do I order?

You can place an order directly through our website by adding items to your cart and checking out securely. Alternatively, we also take orders via Instagram DMs. Once confirmed, you’ll receive an order summary and payment instructions.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept EcoCash USD, cash on collection, Visa, and Mastercard. All payments are securely processed and you’ll receive confirmation upon completion.

Can I pay when I collect my order?

Yes. If you prefer, you can pay by cash or card when collecting your order from our pick-up point.

Are online payment options available?

Absolutely. We accept Visa and Mastercard online, so you can shop from anywhere without needing to pay in person.

Shipping

Where can I collect my order?

Our main collection point is located at 6th Street & Herbert Chitepo Ave in Harare. Once your order is ready, you’ll receive a message with directions and available collection times.

Do you deliver within Harare?

Yes. We offer affordable delivery across Harare, starting at $3 and capped at $5 depending on your location. Delivery times are typically same-day or next-day once payment is confirmed.

How do I track my delivery or collection status?

We’ll keep you updated on your order status via WhatsApp or Instagram DMs. For deliveries, you’ll also receive a driver’s contact and estimated delivery time.
