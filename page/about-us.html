About Us

Bringing creativity, faith, and growth to every design.

The Company

Founded with a passion for art and driven by core values of faith, creativity, and growth, Ballylike is more than just a design brand. We are a community that celebrates individuality, style, and purpose. Each piece is crafted with attention to detail and a commitment to inspire.

Founded with a passion for creating meaningful, visually appealing products, Ballylike blends art with purpose. Whether it's a tote bag, hoodie, or graphic design, our creations aim to stand out and leave a lasting impression. We take pride in infusing every piece with quality, craftsmanship, and a touch of boldness, ensuring that each item is as unique as the person who wears it.

Our Mission

To create innovative and meaningful designs that inspire individuals to live boldly, creatively, and authentically. We are dedicated to providing our customers with high-quality, unique products that reflect their values and personality.

Our Vision

To empower individuals through design, to create a world where creativity, faith, and growth are embraced in every aspect of life. We envision a future where Ballylike products serve as a reminder of the power of authenticity and positive impact.

The Founder

Ballylike was founded by Tirivashe Chitanda, a creator passionate about blending art with values. His mission is to inspire others through the power of design and creative expression.
